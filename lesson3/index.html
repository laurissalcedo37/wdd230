Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is that through technology and science the user of the site can find weather forecasts or the state of the atmosphere in the desired region. It also offers news about how the weather affects some places, either by storms, hurricanes, snowfall, and more. Everything so that the user of the site has complete information about the weather in his region and how it could affect his daily life.
